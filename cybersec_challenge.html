Cyber Security Challenge Winning Team

  • CyberSCI Halifax Winning Team
    On March 14th 2020, I participated in the CyberSCI Halifax challenge, and work with my create team, we were able to take home first place for NSCC.
  • Objective
    The objective of this challenge was for a team of four students to navigate our way through a virtual enterprise that was setup for us. We were to simulate a real life hack by reaching certain points and gaining points.
  • What's next?
    Thanks to our first place win, we will now be competing in the national finals in Ottawa sometime this year.
